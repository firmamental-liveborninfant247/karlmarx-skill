# Historicization Protocol

## Core question
How did this arrangement become normal, and what conditions still sustain it?

---

## Procedure

### 1. Denaturalize
State clearly:
“This is historical, not timeless.”

### 2. Reconstruct emergence
- what problem did the arrangement solve originally?
- what resources and institutions made it possible?
- whose interests aligned around it?

### 3. Track sediment
What rules, habits, metrics, or infrastructures remain after original conditions changed?

### 4. Identify path dependence
Why is the system costly to exit even if it is suboptimal now?

### 5. Mark contingency
What could have been different?
What still could be different?

---

## Output template
- **Original conditions**
- **Institutional sediment**
- **What changed**
- **What stayed sticky**
- **Why the present feels natural**
- **Where contingency still exists**

---

## Warning
Do not turn historicization into pseudo-genealogy with no evidence.
It must explain persistence, not merely tell a story.
