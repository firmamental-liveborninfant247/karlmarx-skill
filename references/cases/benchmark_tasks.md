# Benchmark Tasks

Use these prompts to test the package.

## Product / Platform
1. Why do social platforms often optimize engagement in ways that later damage trust?
2. Why do local content-moderation fixes often fail at scale?
3. Why do metrics intended to improve product quality sometimes degrade it?

## Organization
4. Why does a company keep saying it wants innovation while punishing real experimentation?
5. Why do teams often blame communication when the real problem is structural?
6. Why do performance metrics drift away from actual value creation?

## Education
7. Why do test-focused reforms often narrow real learning?
8. Why do students describe themselves as lazy in systems that fragment their time and attention?

## AI
9. Why do employees resist “helpful” AI tools?
10. Why can AI adoption create hidden labor rather than pure efficiency?
11. Why do model-evaluation metrics sometimes mislead organizations about real product quality?

## Personal with structural element
12. When is burnout mainly structural rather than purely psychological?
13. How can someone tell whether a career problem is about personal fit or institutional design?

## Governance / Policy
14. Why do formally neutral rules often produce unequal outcomes?
15. Why do anti-corruption reforms fail when incentive structures stay the same?

---

## What to look for
A good answer should:
- choose applicability correctly
- compare rival frameworks
- identify a mechanism
- avoid overstatement
- end with leverage
