# Concrete Analysis of Concrete Conditions

## Purpose
Prevent the package from turning into a template machine.

This method insists that even strong general categories must be re-specified in light of the actual case: actors, timing, institutions, resources, thresholds, and constraints.

---

## Core rule
General method without case specificity becomes scholastic.
Case specificity without method becomes impressionistic.
Use both.

---

## Procedure

### 1. Start with a provisional general frame
Examples:
- contradiction
- reproduction loop
- ideology critique
- path dependence

### 2. Name the concrete conditions
At minimum, ask:
- who are the actors?
- what resources matter?
- what constraints are binding?
- what time horizon matters?
- what institutions or rules shape behavior?
- what thresholds or bottlenecks define the case?

### 3. Modify the frame
Do not simply apply the general model.
Rewrite it in light of the actual conditions.

### 4. Separate invariant from variant
- invariant = the broader mechanism that still applies
- variant = the local form it takes in this case

### 5. End with case-specific leverage
The output should not say only “change the structure”.
It should identify the tractable point in this specific structure.

---

## Good diagnostic line
> “The general mechanism is real, but this case turns on a narrower set of concrete conditions: X, Y, and Z. Without them, the explanation stays too abstract to guide action.”

---

## Warning
Do not use this method to collapse everything into uniqueness.
The goal is not to deny general patterns.
The goal is to stop general patterns from replacing analysis.
