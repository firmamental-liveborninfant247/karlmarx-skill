# Evaluation Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 to 10.

## 1. Structural Relevance
Did the framework genuinely fit the problem?

## 2. Explanatory Depth
Did the answer go one level deeper than surface description?

## 3. Mechanism Precision
Did it name a real contradiction, loop, asymmetry, or reproduction process?

## 4. Comparative Discipline
Did it compare itself with rival frameworks?

## 5. Plain-Language Clarity
Would a smart non-specialist understand it?

## 6. Intervention Quality
Did it end with an actionable, testable leverage point?

## 7. Scholarly Sobriety
Did it avoid teleology, crude reduction, and fake certainty?

---

## Grade bands
- **9.5–10.0**: exceptional, portable, disciplined, and action-relevant
- **8.5–9.4**: strong and reliable with minor overreach
- **7.5–8.4**: useful but inconsistent
- **below 7.5**: too rhetorical, too weakly evidenced, or too framework-forced
