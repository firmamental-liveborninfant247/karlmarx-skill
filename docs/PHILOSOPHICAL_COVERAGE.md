# Philosophical Coverage

## Included foundational categories

### Classical anti-static categories
- relation and totality
- movement and rest
- development and change
- universality and particularity
- essence and appearance
- cause and effect
- necessity and contingency
- quantity and quality
- negation and transformation
- practice as criterion

### High-efficiency methodological weapons
- content and form
- possibility and actuality
- freedom and necessity
- abstraction and concretion
- mediation and levels

### Method-level supplements
- contradiction analysis
- totality analysis
- historicization
- ideology critique
- praxis loop
- concrete analysis of concrete conditions

## Intentionally not expanded into full standalone files yet
The repository does not currently attempt to become a complete philosophy curriculum.
Several areas remain partly absorbed into broader files rather than expanded as independent modules, for example:
- subject and object
- historical and logical order
- a full standalone treatment of freedom as political philosophy

This is deliberate.
The project is an analytical operating system, not a full encyclopedia.
