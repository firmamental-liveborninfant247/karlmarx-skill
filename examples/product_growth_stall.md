# Example: Product Growth Stall

## Question
Why does a product with many features still fail to become a daily habit?

## Good framework choices
- relation and totality
- essence and appearance
- mediation and levels
- practice as criterion
- contradiction analysis

## Strong answer shape
- surface: feature richness exists
- structure: value loop is weak or fragmented
- mediation: onboarding, reward timing, social reinforcement, and team KPI logic transmit the problem
- contradiction: feature breadth vs habit depth
- leverage: compress the path to repeatable value, then test retention by cohort
