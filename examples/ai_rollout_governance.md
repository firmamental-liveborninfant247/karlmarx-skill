# Example: AI Rollout and Governance

## Question
Why does an AI deployment look impressive in pilot mode but fail at organization-wide adoption?

## Good framework choices
- possibility and actuality
- mediation and levels
- abstraction and concretion
- historicization
- praxis loop

## Strong answer shape
- surface: pilot success exists
- structure: pilot conditions are not the same as scaled institutional conditions
- possibility/actuality: what worked in principle has not yet become an organization-wide actual possibility
- mediation: policy, incentives, compliance, workflow fit, and ownership transmit the gap
- leverage: change enabling conditions before forcing a broader rollout
