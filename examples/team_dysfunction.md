# Example: Team Dysfunction

## Question
Why is a hardworking team still chronically low-efficiency?

## Good framework choices
- movement and rest
- content and form
- freedom and necessity
- concrete analysis of concrete conditions
- totality analysis

## Strong answer shape
- surface: people appear committed
- structure: incentives, coordination cost, unclear authority, and metric design reproduce inefficiency
- content/form: the team may look collaborative while real coordination remains fragmented
- agency under constraint: individual effort is real but cannot substitute for structural clarity
- leverage: redesign decision rights, interfaces, and work pacing
